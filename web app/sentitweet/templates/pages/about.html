TWITTER DATA SENTIMENT ANALYSIS

Also known as “Opinion Mining”, Sentiment Analysis refers to the use of Natural Language Processing to determine the attitude, opinions and emotions of a speaker, writer, or other subject within an online mention. Essentially, it is the process of determining whether a piece of writing is positive or negative. This is also called the Polarity of the content.
